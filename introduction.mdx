title: "What is OpenCompany?"
description: "Your own AI assistant that does real work - on your machine, with your keys, no subscription"
---

OpenCompany is a free, open-source app that lets you build AI-powered automations by drawing them. Drag boxes onto a canvas, connect them with lines, press **Start** — and your AI answers messages, searches the web, reads files, sends emails, and more. Everything runs on your own computer.

## What you can build

- A chat assistant that answers you in the app — or through a **Telegram bot** you set up in ten minutes.
- A morning digest: read your calendar, have the AI summarize the day, email it to you.
- A research helper: an agent that searches the web on its own and reports back with sources.
- A WhatsApp auto-responder that drafts replies in your tone.
- A scheduled job that watches a folder, summarizes new files, and logs the result.

## Who it's for

**If you've never automated anything:** the [Get Started path](/how-it-works) assumes nothing. Install with two commands, chat with an AI in minutes, then build your first workflow by dragging three boxes.

**If you're a developer:** it's a plugin-first Python/FastAPI backend with a React Flow canvas, Temporal-backed durable execution, and one-folder-per-node plugins. The [Architecture](/architecture) page has the internals, and the [source is on GitHub](https://github.com/zeenie-ai/opencompany).
